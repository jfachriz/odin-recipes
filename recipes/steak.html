Steak

Here's a simple recipe for a delicious steak:

Ingredients:

  • 2 ribeye steaks
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or skillet to high heat.
  2. Season the steaks generously with salt and pepper on both sides.
  3. Drizzle olive oil over the steaks to prevent sticking.
  4. Place the steaks on the grill or skillet and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare, or adjust the time to your desired level of doneness.
  5. Remove the steaks from the heat and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.
